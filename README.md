# They took my cat

An entry to the JS13K 2025 competition, inspired by the classic arcade game Elevator Action.

An evil society of animal snatchers stole your cat! Search their headquarters for Manny the cat and fight dumb agents along the way.

Desktop only. Tested to work in Firefox, Chromium, and WebKitGTK. Safari and other browsers are untested.

## Controls

Move around using arrow keys/WASD:<br>
Left/right to walk.<br>
Down to crouch.<br>
Up/down to control elevators and use doors and stairs.

Space/X to shoot.
